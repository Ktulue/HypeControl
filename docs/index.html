Hype Control

A free Chrome extension that creates intentional friction before Twitch purchases. Stop impulse spending on gift subs, Bits, and subscriptions.

Install from Chrome Web Store Free · Open Source · No data collection

What It Does

Real Cost Breakdown

See the true price with tax, how many work hours it costs, and what else you could buy with that money.

Multi-Step Friction

Four intensity levels from a gentle nudge to a full gauntlet — math challenges, cooldown timers, and type-to-confirm.

Spending Limits

Daily, weekly, and monthly caps with visual progress bars. Auto-escalates friction as you approach your limit.

Streaming Mode

Automatically bypasses friction when you're live on your own channel, so you can gift subs to your community freely.

How It Works

1

Install the extension

Add Hype Control from the Chrome Web Store. Set your hourly rate, tax rate, and friction level.

2

Browse Twitch normally

Hype Control runs silently in the background. No impact until you click a purchase button.

3

Get a reality check

Before any gift sub, subscription, or Bits purchase goes through, you see the real cost — in dollars, work hours, and everyday items.

4

Decide with a clear head

Cancel or proceed — your call. Hype Control tracks your savings and shows you how much you've kept in your pocket.
